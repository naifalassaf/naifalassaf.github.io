A Little About Myself

Hi, I am Naif! Born and raised in the Riyadh, Saudi Arabia, I have always been interested in computers. Be it text editors or video games, I have always loved exploring anything on our family's PC. I moved to the US in 2018 for college, got a bachelor's degree majoring Computer Science and a minor in Space in May 2022, and now I am developing my resume and practicing my writing in my spare time. My dream is to make tools that make people lives easier, happier, and more fun.

Other than Coding, Cooking, and Tabletop Games, I am interested in astronomy, Reading, and storytelling.
