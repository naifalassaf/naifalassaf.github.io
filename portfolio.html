Some projects I worked on

Maze Solving Robot

Simulated in Webots, our robot traverses a maze using a birds-eye photograph of it. We used blob detection to isolate the maze and map it for the robot’s use. The robot uses Dijkstra’s algorithm to solve for the optimal path.

Updating and maintaining a mobile application

Worked with CU boulder Psychology department’s Correll lab to update and modernise their CRD (Cross Racial Deficit) app. Our team, streamlined deployment, wrote documentation, updated the UI, and gamified the app.
